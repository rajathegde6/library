6.DOAB
The Directory of Open Access Books is a service of OAPEN Foundation. The OAPEN Foundation is an international
initiative dedicated to Open Access monograph publishing, based at the National Library in The Hague. The primary
aim of DOAB is to increase discoverability of Open Access books. The directory will be open to all publishers
who publish academic, peer reviewed books in Open Access and should contain as many books as possible, provided that
these publications are in Open Access and meet academic standards.
http://www.doabooks.org/
